Classic blue-tech look with clean icy contrast.

4. Code Editor

Moody but readable with dusky reds and pale ink.

7. Funky Neon

High-energy pop with strong complementary contrast.

8. Metal Forge

Steel grey and ember orange, industrial and heavy.

9. Paper Craft

Soft hand-made paper tones with warm natural balance.

10. Ocean Pop

Bright sea blues and mint foam, fresh and friendly.
